Research Labs

What does it mean to do research @IIITD? Visit some of our research labs and interact with research scholars, learn about the area!

  • 1. Image Analysis and Biometrics Research Lab     


    • Image Analysis and Biometrics Research Lab
    (Academic Building, 2nd floor, A wing)

    The Image Analysis and Biometrics Lab is working on developing novel solutions for biometrics related problems inspired from image analysis, pattern classification, and machine learning paradigms. During the Open House -2015, we will share some interesting biometric research projects that the students and faculty members of this lab are pursuing at IIIT-Delhi. Some representative examples are latent fingerprint recognition, iris recognition, face recognition, kinect, and understanding human perception.
    https://research.iiitd.edu.in/groups/iab/index.html

  • 2. Cybersecurity Education and Research Centre (CERC) 


    • Cybersecurity Education and Research Centre (CERC) 
    (Academic Building,4th Wing)

    Our mission is to: Build systems and tools that are of direct interest to different stakeholders (Government, Industry, Citizens); Create a pipeline for Undergraduate, Masters, and Ph.D. students working in related areas; Conduct high-end technical training on cybersecurity for Government of India officials and corporations in topics such as network security, distributed systems security, multi media security, secure coding, protecting critical infrastructure, privacy and security in online social media, and so on; Create awareness among public about Cybersecurity and privacy in India.
    http://cerc.iiitd.ac.in

  • 3. RF and Applied Electromagnetics Lab        


    • RF and Applied Electromagnetics Lab 
    (L31, Library Building, 3rd floor)

    ​The RF and Applied Electromagnetics Lab has the resources for advanced teaching and research in the area of RF, Antenna, and Radar. The attendees will be shown the dual-band component design approaches using Advanced Design System (ADS). Thereafter, network analyser measurement of some of the recently designed dual-band RF components will be demonstrated.
    http://www.iiitd.ac.in/facilities/labs/rfael_lab

  • 4. Circuits and VLSI Lab                


    • Circuits and VLSI Lab 
    (L33, Library Building, 3rd floor)

    This teaching/ research laboratory is established to investigate various aspects of VLSI circuits and systems design. Specifically we are interested in design of energy efficient digital circuits and systems, embedded systems, sensor development etc.
    http://www.iiitd.ac.in/facilities/labs/cvl_lab

  • 5. Cryptography Research Lab             


    • Cryptography Research Lab 
    (Academic building, 4rd Floor, A wing 4A lab)

    The Cryptography Research Lab aims at evaluating and developing various secure systems using cryptographic techniques. Our lab members are two faculty, nine PhDs, and many more Mtech and Btech students. Our research area covers HW & SW implementation security, design and evaluation of new and existing cryptographic algorithms and protocols, privacy-enhancing technology, development of analysis tools, etc.

  • 6. Communication Research Lab            


    • Communication Research Lab 
    (Library building, 3rd Floor, L32)

    The communication system lab aims at bridging the gap between theoretical simulation and implementation aspects of a communication system.  In principle, the students in this lab design and implement the techniques they have learned during their lectures. The demonstrations that will be shown to the visitors, include a wireless communication link between two software defined radios, a glimpse of a Microsoft spectrum observatory and Spectrum sensing using national instruments boards.
